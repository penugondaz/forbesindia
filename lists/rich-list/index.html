Methodology & Notes

How we compile the list: The Forbes India Rich List 2025 ranks individuals by their estimated net worth as of a specific cut-off date. We consider publicly traded stocks, closely held businesses, real estate, art, and other assets, with debt subtracted. All figures are in US dollars.

The list includes Indian citizens residing in India or abroad, as well as Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) with significant business ties to India. Currency conversions are based on exchange rates at the time of valuation.

Sources: Our researchers draw on stock exchanges, regulatory filings, interviews, and third-party valuations. Relatives are counted separately unless they share the same business portfolio.

Wealth change reflects movement vs the previous year. New = first-time entrant. Returnee = previously listed. Split = wealth formally divided among family members.

Note: This list was compiled using data available up to August 31, 2025. Forbes India makes every effort to publish accurate and complete information; figures represent an estimate of known wealth and should not be construed as an exhaustive account of any individual's total assets. The ranking is based solely on net worth and does not represent any endorsement or assessment of character.
