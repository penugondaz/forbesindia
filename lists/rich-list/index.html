Methodology & Notes

How we compile the list: The Forbes India Rich List 2025 ranks individuals by their estimated net worth as of a specific cut-off date. We consider the value of publicly traded stocks, closely held businesses, real estate, art, yachts, planes, and other assets. Debt is subtracted to arrive at the net worth figure.

All wealth figures are in US dollars. Currency conversions are based on exchange rates at the time of valuation. The list includes Indian citizens residing in India or abroad, as well as Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) with significant business ties to India.

Sources and verification: Our researchers draw on stock exchanges, regulatory filings, interviews with the listees and their representatives, and third-party valuations. We do not include estimates of wealth that cannot be independently verified. Relatives are counted separately unless they share the same business interests and/or portfolio.

Wealth change reflects the change in net worth compared to the previous year's list. New denotes a first-time entrant; Returnee denotes someone who has appeared on a previous list but was absent last year. Split indicates the wealth has been formally divided among family members.

Note: This list was compiled using data available up to August 31, 2025. Forbes India makes every effort to publish accurate and complete information, but we cannot guarantee the accuracy of third-party data. The figures reported here represent an estimate of known wealth and should not be construed as an exhaustive account of any individual's total assets. The ranking is solely based on net worth and does not represent any endorsement or assessment of character.
