Hello and welcome back to Forbes India Weekly Digest!

No breakthrough from the 21-hour long Iran-US ceasefire talks doesn’t bode well.

Though the negotiations held in Islamabad opened a window into a possible agreement, the long road to a truce will continue to have an inflationary impact globally.

World food prices were the highest in March 2026 since September last year, impacted by rising energy cost. The United Nations Food and Agriculture Organisation said that if the conflict continues for 40 days, it will have an impact on commodity prices and food supply for the rest of the year.

India is already taking a cut on fuel taxes to protect consumers, even as international crude oil prices skyrocket. The latest South Asia report by World Bank has projected a 6.6 percent growth in FY27 for India’s economy, down from 7.6 percent in FY26 due to the global energy disruptions, rising costs, and eroding income.

However, global trade deals and strong domestic demands continue to fuel the fastest-growing major economy in the world.

🏥Ajit and Sarah Isaac’s focus on dignity of life

Driven by their experience, Ajit and Sarah Isaac’s philanthropic efforts started with a focus on dignity of life through palliative and geriatric care. Through the Thankamma Ithapiri Memorial Trust (TIMT) and the Ajit Isaac Foundation (AIF), their initiative cares for the old, ailing and infirm – an area where India has very little infrastructure and support. They also fund paediatric care facilities at Christian Medical College in Vellore.

💉Generics break Mounjaro’s hold on the market

It is happening -- Eli Lilly’s Mounjaro witnessed a drop in sales by a factor of Rs 20 crore for the month of March, 2026. The sales of semaglutide class of drugs for weight loss however continued to rise, with generics jostling for space on the counter.

➤Branded generics from Torrent, Dr Reddy’s, Zydus, Lupin, and Sun Pharma are now available for sale in India.

➤Torrent’s oral semaglutide already accounts for 3 percent of the Rs 1,600 crore market.

➤To stay competitive in the market, Eli Lilly has already improved distribution through a tie-up with Cipla and the launch of its tirzepatide drug for weight loss.

👔The role of independent directors expands beyond mere figureheads

Independent directors today occupy critical positions across audit, risk, and remuneration committees – a welcome change from figureheads of yesteryears. The resignation of part-time chairman and independent director at HDFC Bank, Atanu Chakraborty, shines the spotlight on the power and remit of an independent director.

➤The average remuneration for independent directors at Nifty50 has nearly doubled from FY20 to FY25 according to reports.

➤Exit of independent directors has become signals of impending scrutiny or financial stress.

➤Over the years, markets regulator Sebi has tightened the norms around independence, tenure and disclosure.

🍜Eating out is now intentional and curated by supper clubs

The rise of supper clubs in urban India offers intimate, curated dining experience to the discerning gastronome, while fostering a community. Focus on a single cuisine or culture makes supper clubs an experience for the gram.

➤Attending an invitation-only supper club costs between Rs 2500 to Rs 4500 per person with no two meals being the same.

➤Supper club experiences offer high touch, screen free environment for mindful eating.

➤These experiences also become a means of storytelling, cultural exchange, and an avenue to make friends as adults.
